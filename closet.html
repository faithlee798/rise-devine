Organization


Organization can be integrated into your life everyday in different ways.



You can organize your closet, bedroom, desk, backpack, etc.!


It is important to be organized so you can optimize space as well as not be stressed about clutter!


Closet Organization

Having an organized closet is one of the easiest ways to look and feel your best everyday. Being organized allows you to be able to easily find exactly what you are looking for and craft the perfect outfit for your day. There are many ways to do this so choose what works best for you!



Photo courtesy of RealSimple.

An easy way to organize your closet is by color. This is useful if you like to match certain colors with others, or if you just want to easily find certain pieces of clothes.

Another way to organize is by "season". This means keeping clothes you usually wear in the cold in one place, and clothes you wear for warmer weather in a different section.

This is similar to organizing by type of clothes. For example, keeping pants,
shorts, sweatshirts, shirts, pajamas, etc. seperated. You can
also organize by grouping casual clothes in one area, and more fancy
clothes in another.


You should go through your closet every few months and clean out clothes you don't wear.

You can then donate those clothes to a donation center near you!
